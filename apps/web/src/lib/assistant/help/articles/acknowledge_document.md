When an admin uploads a document with acknowledgment required, you'll see a prompt in the Documents section and receive reminder emails until you acknowledge it.

1. Open **Documents** from the left sidebar.
2. Find the document with a **Pending Acknowledgment** badge.
3. Click the document name to open or download it and read the content.
4. Click the **Acknowledge** button.

The badge disappears and you won't receive any more reminders for that document. Your acknowledgment timestamp is recorded for the admin's compliance tracking.
