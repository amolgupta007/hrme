# Add a payroll line item

## Steps

1. Open **Payroll** and find the relevant run (must be **processed**, not yet paid).
2. Click the row for the employee to open the **edit dialog**.
3. In the **Line items** card, pick a **category**: Bonus, Allowance, Reimbursement, or Other.
4. Enter the **amount** (whole rupees) and a short **note** (optional).
5. Toggle **Taxable** off for reimbursements (non-taxable items add to net pay without TDS).
6. Click **Add**.

Line items are recomputed into the entry's TDS + net pay immediately.
Remove an item by clicking the ✕ next to it.
