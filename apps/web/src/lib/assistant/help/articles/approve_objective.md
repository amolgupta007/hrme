When a direct report submits their objectives, you'll see them in your approval queue. Review the items and either approve or return them for revision.

1. Open **Objectives** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **To Approve** tab to see all submitted objectives waiting for your action.
3. Click on an objective to read the items and targets.
4. Click **Approve** to lock the objective in, or **Send Back** to request changes.
5. If sending back, add a **Comment** explaining what needs to change — the employee sees this.

Approved objectives move to Active status and can no longer be edited by the employee. Returned objectives go back to Draft so the employee can revise and resubmit.
