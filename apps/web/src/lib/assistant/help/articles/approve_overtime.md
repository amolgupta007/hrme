# Approve or reject overtime

## Steps

1. Open **Attendance → Overtime** tab (visible only when overtime is enabled).
2. Filter by status (Pending / Approved / Rejected / Pushed).
3. For each pending record:
   - Click the **✅ tick** to approve.
   - Click the **✖ cross**, enter a reason, and click **Reject** to reject.
4. To approve many at once, tick checkboxes and click **Approve N selected**.

Approved OT can then be pushed to the relevant month's payroll run.
