Admins manage the course library and enrol employees in training. You can mark courses as mandatory and set due dates to track compliance.

1. Open **Training** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Courses** tab.
3. Click **New Course** to create one, or click an existing course name to open it.
4. On the course detail page, click **Enrol Employees**.
5. Select the employees you want to enrol — you can filter by department.
6. Set a **Due Date** for completion.
7. Click **Enrol** to assign the course.

Enrolled employees see the course in their **My Training** tab immediately. Overdue courses are flagged automatically and you'll get a reminder email if completions are lagging.
