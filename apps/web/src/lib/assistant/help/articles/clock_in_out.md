The attendance page lets you record when you start and finish work each day. You can only have one open shift per day.

1. Open **Attendance** from the left sidebar.
2. If you haven't clocked in yet today, click the **Clock In** button.
3. The button changes to **Clock Out** and your start time is shown.
4. When you're done for the day, click **Clock Out**.

Your total working hours for the day are calculated automatically. If you forget to clock out, the system will auto-close your shift at midnight IST using your organisation's standard workday hours setting. The auto-close is shown with a note on your attendance history.
