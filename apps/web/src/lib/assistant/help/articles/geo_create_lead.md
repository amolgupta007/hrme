1. Go to **JambaGeo → Leads**.
2. Click **New lead** in the top-right.
3. Fill in the **name** (required). Company, phone, email, address, value, and source are optional.
4. Pick a **source** from the dropdown — Walk-in, Referral, WhatsApp, IndiaMART, JustDial, Google, Facebook, Direct, or Other. Picking from the list keeps reports clean; free-text was retired so the funnel doesn't collapse under spelling drift like "Walk-in / walkin / Walk in" being counted as three different sources.
5. Pick a **stage** (defaults to New) and optionally assign to a staff member.
6. Click **Create lead**.

If you assigned the lead, the assignee gets an email immediately. Managers can only assign leads to staff in their own department.
