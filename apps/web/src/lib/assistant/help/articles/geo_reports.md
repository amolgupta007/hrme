1. Go to **JambaGeo → Reports**.
2. The **Funnel** chart shows how many leads are in each pipeline stage.
3. The **Conversion rate** metric shows what percentage of leads reached Closed.
4. The **Overdue follow-ups** list shows leads where the next follow-up date has passed without a logged visit.
5. Use the **date range** picker to filter by creation date or last activity.
6. Managers see only leads assigned to their department. Admins and owners see all leads.

Export is not available in Phase 1. Use the filter bar on the Leads page for CSV-style views.
