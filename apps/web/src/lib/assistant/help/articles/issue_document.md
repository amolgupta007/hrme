Once a template is active you can issue it to employees. Each employee's variables auto-fill from their record, and you choose which legal entity issues the document.

1. Open **Offer Letters** from the sidebar, then the **Issue** tab.
2. Choose an active **template** and the **issuing entity** (if your organisation is part of a company group, pick which entity's name appears on the document).
3. Click **Next**, then select one or more employees to issue to.
4. Click **Preview** to review each document. Variables auto-fill from the employee record and salary structure — edit any value inline before sending.
5. Anything shown as `[variable]` means no value was found; fill it in before sending.
6. Click **Send** to email each employee a secure acknowledgement link.

Employees open the link, review the document, and type their name to acknowledge. The link expires after 30 days. You can track delivery and responses on the **Signed Records** tab.
