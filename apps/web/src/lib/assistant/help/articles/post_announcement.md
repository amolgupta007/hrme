Announcements are a quick way to share company-wide news, policy updates, or event notices with the whole team.

1. Open **Announcements** from the left sidebar.
2. Click **New Announcement**.
3. Enter a clear **Title** for the announcement.
4. Choose a **Category**: General, Policy, Event, or Urgent.
5. Write the announcement **Body** in the text area — Markdown formatting is supported.
6. Toggle **Pin to top** on if this is important enough to stay at the top of the feed.
7. Click **Publish**.

The announcement is immediately visible to all employees in the Announcements section. Pinned announcements appear above unpinned ones regardless of date. Admins can edit or delete any announcement after publishing.
