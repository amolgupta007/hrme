You can submit a leave request for any leave type that your company has configured. Your manager and any org admins will be notified by email.

1. Open **Leave** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **New Request** tab.
3. Choose the **Leave Type** (Paid, Sick, Casual, etc.) from the dropdown.
4. Pick your **Start Date** and **End Date** using the date pickers.
5. Add an optional **Reason** if you want to share context with your manager.
6. Click **Submit Request**.

Your request appears under the **My Requests** tab with a Pending status. You'll get an email when your manager approves or rejects it. The days are deducted from your balance only after approval.
