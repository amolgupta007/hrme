The Signed Records tab shows the status of every issued document and the append-only audit trail of acknowledgements. It's visible to owners and admins only.

1. Open **Offer Letters** from the sidebar, then the **Signed Records** tab.
2. The **Issued documents** section lists every document and its status: draft, sent, viewed, acknowledged, or declined. Click the file icon to preview the draft PDF.
3. The **Signed records** section lists each acknowledgement, with the signer's name, entity, template, timestamp, and capture method.
4. Use the entity filter to narrow records when your group issues from multiple entities.
5. Click the download icon on any signed record to open the final signed PDF, which embeds the acknowledgement block (name, timestamp, and IP address).

Signed records are immutable — they can never be edited or deleted, so they serve as a reliable audit trail. The acknowledgement is an electronic record of receipt and agreement, not a digitally certified or Aadhaar-based signature.
