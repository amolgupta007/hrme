A review cycle activates self-assessments and manager reviews for the whole org during a defined period. You can run multiple cycles per year (e.g. mid-year and annual).

1. Open **Reviews** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Cycles** tab.
3. Click **New Cycle**.
4. Enter a **Cycle Name** (e.g. "Mid-Year 2026"), then set the **Start Date** and **End Date**.
5. Choose the review template or leave it as default.
6. Click **Launch Cycle**.

Once launched, the cycle status changes to Active. All employees can immediately begin their self-assessments and managers see their team's reviews in their queue.
