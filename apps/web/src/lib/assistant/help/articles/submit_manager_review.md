Once your direct report submits their self-review, you can complete the manager assessment. Both sides are combined into one review record.

1. Open **Reviews** from the left sidebar.
2. Click on an active cycle to see your team's review list.
3. Click **Review** next to the employee whose assessment you want to complete.
4. Read through their self-assessment ratings and comments in the left panel.
5. Fill in your **Manager Rating** for each competency and add **Manager Comments**.
6. Click **Submit Manager Review**.

The review is now complete. The employee can view both sides. You can edit your manager assessment up until the cycle end date. You cannot review someone who is not in your reporting chain.
