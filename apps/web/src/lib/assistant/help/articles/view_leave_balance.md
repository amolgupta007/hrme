Your leave balance shows how many days you have left for each leave type this year. It updates in real time as requests are approved.

1. Open **Leave** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **My Balance** tab.
3. Review the balance cards — each card shows Total Days, Used Days, and Remaining Days for one leave type.
4. Check the **Carried Forward** value if your company allows carry-over from the previous year.

Balances reflect approved and pending requests. If a pending request is later rejected, the days are returned to your balance automatically.
