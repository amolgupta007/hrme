Your assigned training courses appear in the Training section. Complete them by the due date to stay compliant.

1. Open **Training** from the left sidebar.
2. You'll see your assigned courses with due dates and current status (Assigned, In Progress, or Completed).
3. Click a course card to open the course details and external learning link.
4. As you work through the course, click **Update Progress** and enter your completion percentage.
5. When you've finished, set progress to 100% and click **Mark Complete**.
6. Download your **Certificate** from the course detail page once the status shows Completed.

Overdue courses are highlighted in red. Contact your admin if you need a due date extension or if a course is incorrectly assigned to you.
