Managers and admins can see a live view of who is present and who hasn't clocked in yet for the current day.

1. Open **Attendance** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Team Today** tab.
3. You'll see a table of all employees with their clock-in time, clock-out time (if applicable), and current status (In / Out / Not Clocked In).
4. Use the **Department** filter to narrow down to a specific team.

The view refreshes when you reload the page. Employees who are on approved leave today are shown with a Leave tag so you don't confuse absence with truancy.
