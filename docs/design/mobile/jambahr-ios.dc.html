USAGE RULES

Green is the only brand color. One primary CTA per screen. JambaHire indigo (#5B5BD6) appears only inside the hiring module — never mix the two on one screen.

Status colors always sit on their tint, never as solid fills except the Approved pill and count badges. Red is reserved for overdue/rejected/deductions — not decoration.

Money is monospaced (SF Mono), Indian digit grouping (₹2,51,200), deductions in danger red with a leading −. Cards over #F7F7F4 canvas; avoid stacked shadows.
