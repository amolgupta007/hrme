Employee Leave Policy
Human Resources · Applicable to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees in India operations.

1. Purpose & Scope

This policy establishes the leave entitlements, application procedures, and approval guidelines for all employees at Medialoop Communications Pvt. Ltd. It is designed to support a healthy work-life balance while ensuring uninterrupted delivery to our clients across broadcast, digital, and OOH verticals.

This policy applies to all employees based in India. Employees on deputation or international assignments are governed by separate addenda issued by the HR team.

3. Annual / Earned Leave

3.1 Accrual

Earned Leave accrues at 1.75 days per calendar month from the date of confirmation into a permanent role. Leave balance is updated on the 1st of each month in the HR portal.

3.2 Application

  • Apply at least 7 calendar days in advance for planned leaves of 3 or more days.
  • For single-day leaves, a minimum of 1 working day advance notice is required.
  • All applications must be submitted through the HR portal. Verbal or WhatsApp approvals are not valid.

3.3 Approval

Reporting managers must approve or decline leave requests within 2 working days of submission. Unanswered requests beyond 3 working days shall be escalated to the Department Head automatically by the system.

4. Sick Leave

Sick Leave is granted for genuine medical needs. An employee availing more than 3 consecutive sick days must submit a medical certificate from a registered practitioner upon return.

Sick Leave cannot be prefixed or suffixed with a weekend or a public holiday without prior written approval from HR. Misuse of sick leave may lead to disciplinary action.

5. Casual Leave

Casual Leave is intended for unforeseen personal exigencies. It cannot be taken for more than 2 consecutive days at a stretch. Combining Casual Leave with other leave types (except public holidays) requires HR approval.

6. Maternity & Paternity Leave

6.1 Maternity

Medialoop fully complies with the Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2017. Eligible female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of fully paid maternity leave for the first two children. For the third child onwards, the entitlement is 12 weeks.

Employees are also entitled to a work-from-home arrangement for up to 3 months post-leave, subject to the nature of the role, at the discretion of the department head.

6.2 Paternity

Male employees are entitled to 5 paid working days of paternity leave, to be taken within 30 days of the birth of the child.

7. Leave Without Pay (LWP)

If an employee exhausts all applicable leave quotas, further absence will be treated as Leave Without Pay. LWP impacts monthly payroll on a pro-rata basis and will be reflected in the salary slip.

For leaves that exceed available balance, employees must raise a Leave Ticket in the HR portal, which will be reviewed manually by the Department Head and HR Business Partner.

8. Public Holidays

Medialoop observes 10 mandatory public holidays and 2 optional/restricted holidays per leave year, as published in the Annual Holiday Calendar by HR before 31 March each year. Employees working on a mandatory public holiday are eligible for a Comp-off Leave credit.

9. Responsibilities

Role Responsibility
Employee Apply in advance, ensure handover before availing leave, update status on HR portal.
Reporting Manager Review and approve/reject within 2 working days. Ensure team coverage during leaves.
HR Team Maintain leave records, handle disputes, update policy, publish holiday calendar.
Finance Process LWP deductions and leave encashment at separation.

10. Policy Violations

Misrepresentation of leave reasons, falsification of medical certificates, or unauthorised absences will be treated as a disciplinary matter and may result in termination of employment as per the Medialoop Code of Conduct.

11. Policy Review & Amendments

This policy is reviewed annually by the HR team in consultation with senior management. Amendments are communicated to all employees at least 30 days before the effective date via the HR portal and company email.
