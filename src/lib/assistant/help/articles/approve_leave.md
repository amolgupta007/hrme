JambaHR routes new leave requests to every manager and admin in the org. You'll see them by email and in the dashboard.

1. Open **Leave** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Pending** tab.
3. Find the request you want to action.
4. Click **Approve** (green) or **Reject** (red).
5. If you reject, add an optional comment — the requester sees this in their email.

Approvals fire an email to the requester within about a minute. Their leave balance updates immediately. You cannot approve your own request — those go to your own manager or to an admin.
