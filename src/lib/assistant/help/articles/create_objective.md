Objectives let you document what you're working towards this quarter or year. Your manager approves them before they're locked in.

1. Open **Objectives** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **My Objectives** tab, then click **New Objective**.
3. Choose the **Period Type** (quarterly or annual) and the **Period Label** (e.g. "Q2 2026").
4. Add your objective items — click **Add Item** for each key result or goal.
5. Fill in each item's description and target.
6. Click **Submit for Approval** when you're happy with the draft.

Your manager sees the objective in their **To Approve** tab. You'll get notified when they approve or send it back with comments. You can edit the objective while it's still in Draft status.
