keywords: [assign lead, reassign, owner, staff, field rep]
---

1. Go to **JambaGeo → Leads**.
2. Find the lead card in the kanban or use the search/filter bar.
3. Click the lead card to open the detail panel.
4. In the **Assignee** field, pick a staff member from the dropdown.
5. Click **Save**.

The new assignee receives an email notification. The previous assignee is no longer associated with the lead. Managers can only assign leads to employees in their own department.
