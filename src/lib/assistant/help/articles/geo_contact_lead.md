Every place a lead's phone or email appears in JambaGeo is a one-tap contact action — no copy-paste into another app.

**Phone (tap-to-dial)**:

1. Open any lead card on the kanban, any row on the leads list, or the lead detail page.
2. Tap the phone number. On a phone or tablet this opens the dialer with the number prefilled. On a laptop it opens whatever your OS has registered for `tel:` links (FaceTime, Skype, your softphone, etc.) — or nothing if no handler is registered.

**WhatsApp**:

1. Lead cards and the detail page show a small green **WhatsApp** chip right next to the phone number.
2. Tap it to open the contact in WhatsApp (web, desktop, or mobile depending on your device).
3. JambaGeo does not guess a country code — it sends whatever digits are stored. If you've stored "9876543210" without +91, WhatsApp may open the contact picker instead of jumping straight to the chat.

**Email**:

1. On the lead detail page, tap the email address to open your default mail app with the address prefilled.

The kanban card uses both — tapping the phone or WhatsApp chip dials or opens WhatsApp without navigating to the lead detail; tapping anywhere else on the card opens the lead.
