1. Go to **JambaGeo → Leads**.
2. The board shows six columns: New → Contacted → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed.
3. **Drag and drop** a lead card to a different column to advance or revert its stage.
4. Alternatively, open the lead detail panel and use the **Stage** dropdown to pick a stage directly.

The stage change is saved immediately. You can move a lead to any stage in any direction — there are no forced forward-only rules. Employees can only move leads assigned to them; admins and managers can move any lead.
