1. Go to **JambaGeo → Leads** and click the lead you visited.
2. In the lead detail panel, click **Log visit**.
3. Select the **outcome** (In progress, Pending, Follow-up, Converted, or Lost).
4. Add **notes** about what happened.
5. Optionally set a **next follow-up date**.
6. Click **Save visit**.

The visit is added to the lead's timeline. If the outcome is **Converted** or **Lost**, the lead stage updates automatically. Overdue follow-ups appear in the Reports tab.
