Every lead detail page now answers the question "when do I follow up?" without making you scan the visit history.

**Seeing the next follow-up:**

1. Open a lead from the kanban or list.
2. On the **Lead details** card, look for the **Next follow-up** row near the top.
3. If a future follow-up is scheduled, you'll see the date, the relative day ("In 3 days" or "Tomorrow"), and the assignee who logged it.
4. If no follow-up is scheduled, the row reads "No follow-up scheduled" with a **Schedule one** link.

**Scheduling a follow-up without logging a full visit:**

1. Click the **Schedule one** link on the Next follow-up row.
2. Pick a date.
3. Add an optional note explaining what the follow-up is about.
4. Click **Schedule**.

The follow-up appears in the lead's visit timeline as an audit row with outcome "Follow-up" so the history stays honest. To schedule a follow-up while also logging what just happened, use **Log visit** instead — the visit dialog has a follow-up date field too.

To see leads with a follow-up date that's already passed, open **Reports** — the "Overdue follow-ups" card lists them with days-overdue counts.
