JambaGeo is the lead-CRM and field-staff tracking module. In Phase 1 you can:

- Create leads, assign them to staff, and move them through a 6-stage kanban: **New → Contacted → Visited → Negotiation → Converted → Lost**.
- Log visits with outcomes (in progress, pending, follow-up, converted, lost). A "converted" or "lost" outcome auto-advances the lead's stage.
- Contact a lead in one tap: phone numbers are tap-to-dial, emails open your mail app, and a WhatsApp icon next to the phone opens `wa.me` directly.
- Configure geofences around your office and client sites on a map.
- See lead funnel reports and overdue follow-ups.

The mobile app for field staff (consent-first GPS pings, on-the-go check-in/out) ships in a later phase. Until then, the Live Map tab shows a "coming soon" state.

JambaGeo is on the **Business plan**. An admin can enable it in **Settings → JambaGeo**.

If your org has zero leads, the Leads page shows a first-run screen with an "Add your first lead" button. Any manager or admin can use it to kick things off.
