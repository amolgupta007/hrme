# Send payslip emails

Payslip emails are sent automatically when you mark a payroll run as **Paid**.
You can also re-send or send on-demand:

## Steps

1. Open **Payroll** and find a **processed** or **paid** run.
2. Click **Send payslips** in the run row.
3. The toast shows how many were sent and how many failed.

Failed sends are recorded against each entry — re-clicking **Send payslips** retries only the failed entries.
