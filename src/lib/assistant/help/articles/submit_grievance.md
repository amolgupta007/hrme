The grievance module lets you raise a concern with HR or management. You can choose to submit anonymously — your identity will not be recorded or recoverable.

1. Open **Grievances** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Submit** tab.
3. Choose the **Type** of grievance (Harassment, Compensation, Work Environment, etc.).
4. Select the **Severity** (Low, Medium, High, or Urgent).
5. Toggle **Submit Anonymously** on if you don't want your name attached.
6. Write a clear description of the issue in the **Details** field.
7. Click **Submit Grievance**.

You'll see a unique **Tracking Token** (e.g. GRV-XXXXXX) on the confirmation screen — copy and save it. Use it on the **Track Status** tab to check updates without logging in. Anonymous submissions do not appear in **My Submissions**.
