During an active review cycle, you'll complete a self-assessment covering your performance ratings, comments, and goals. Your manager reviews this alongside their own assessment.

1. Open **Reviews** from the left sidebar.
2. Find the active cycle card and click **Start Self-Review** (or **Continue** if you've already begun).
3. Fill in your **Self Rating** for each competency using the 1–5 scale.
4. Add **Comments** in the text fields to give context for your ratings.
5. List your key **Goals** for the next period in the goals section.
6. Click **Submit Review** when you're done.

After submission you can view but not edit your self-review. Your manager can now complete their side of the assessment. You'll be notified when the manager review is published.
