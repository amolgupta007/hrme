Admins see all grievances — including anonymous ones — in the Inbox tab. Update the status and add notes to keep the reporter informed.

1. Open **Grievances** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Inbox** tab to see all submitted grievances.
3. Click a grievance row to open the detail view.
4. Review the type, severity, and description.
5. Update the **Status** (Open → In Review → Resolved or Closed) using the dropdown.
6. Add an **Admin Note** — non-anonymous reporters see this when they track their grievance via the token.
7. Click **Save Changes**.

Anonymous grievances show no employee name. Status updates and notes are visible to the reporter only through the Track Status page using their token. You cannot de-anonymise a submission.
