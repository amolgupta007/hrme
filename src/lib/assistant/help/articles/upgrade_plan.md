Upgrading unlocks additional modules like Documents, Reviews, Training (Growth) or Payroll and JambaHire (Business). Payment is in INR via Razorpay.

1. Open **Settings** from the left sidebar.
2. Scroll to the **Billing** section.
3. Click **Upgrade to Growth** or **Upgrade to Business** depending on the plan you want.
4. The Razorpay checkout modal opens — enter your card or UPI details and complete payment.
5. Wait about 60 seconds after payment for the webhook to update your plan.
6. Refresh the page — your new plan name appears in the billing section and new features unlock in the sidebar.

If the plan doesn't update within 2–3 minutes, try refreshing again. Contact support at support@jambahr.com if the issue persists. You can manage or cancel your subscription from the same Billing section.
