Admins can upload documents and share them with the whole company or specific employees. You can also require employees to acknowledge they have read the document.

1. Open **Documents** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Upload** tab.
3. Click **Choose File** and select the document from your computer.
4. Enter a **Document Name** and choose a **Category** (Policy, Contract, ID, Tax, or Certificate).
5. Toggle **Company-wide** on to make it visible to all employees, or leave it off to assign it to specific people.
6. Toggle **Require Acknowledgment** if employees must confirm they have read it.
7. Click **Upload**.

The document appears in the document list immediately. If acknowledgment is required, employees will see a prompt and receive a weekly reminder email until they acknowledge.
