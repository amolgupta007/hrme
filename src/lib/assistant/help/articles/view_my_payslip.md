Your payslips are available as soon as your admin processes the monthly payroll run. You can view them at any time and print or save them as PDFs.

1. Open **Payroll** from the left sidebar.
2. Click the **My Payslips** tab.
3. You'll see a list of months with processed payslips. Click the month you want.
4. The payslip opens showing your gross, all deductions (PF, TDS, PT), and net pay.
5. Click **Print / Save as PDF** — your browser's print dialog opens. Choose **Save as PDF** as the destination.

Payslips are only available for months where the payroll run has been Processed or Paid. Contact your admin if a month is missing or if there's a discrepancy in the figures.
