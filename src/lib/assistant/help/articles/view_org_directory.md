The company directory shows all active employees in your organisation. Anyone who is logged in can browse it to find a colleague's role, department, or contact details.

1. Open **Directory** from the left sidebar.
2. Use the **Search** box to find someone by name or email.
3. Filter by **Department** or **Role** using the dropdowns to narrow results.
4. Click an employee card to see their full profile — department, manager, employment type, and work email.

The directory only shows employees with Active status. Terminated employees are not listed.
